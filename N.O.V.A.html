KAL WARDIN

Hello! My name is Captain Kal Wardin and I am your host on this website created just for fun. Let me first tell you about myself. I a marine hero who was enjoying retirement. After the Xenos attack Earth and its colonies, I was forced back into active duty with Near Orbit Vanguard Alliance aka N.O.V.A.

N.O.V.A.

I was always known as a good soldier for the Near Orbit Vanguard Alliance

,even from the time I was in boot camp. But nobody knew just how good a soldier I was until the War of Judgment.
My legend would begin at that frightful time in history when a Colonial Administration spaceship unexpectedly
returned from a mission. The vessel was locked on a direct course for Earth and ignored all attempts at communication.
Unsure what fate befell the crew, N.O.V.A. command decided to expect the worst.



Recreational Colony

At the time, I was retired and living a quiet life in a recreational colony New Ceres. But, as my services were needed by N.O.V.A. once more.
While enjoying a drink, I was approached by Bonesy and Clydesdale. They forcibly returned me to N.O.V.A. service by order of Governor Lysenko
to help repel the Xeno invasion. Yelena, a computer AI, helped me adjust to my Mobile Armored Suit by making me pan around and walk. She then
led me to a N.O.V.A. Shuttle. When I arrived at the helipad, a few Grunts came out of portals and attacked me. After I killed them I got in the Shuttle
and flew to the wrecked Colonial Pride.



Colonial Pride

I searched the ship, looking for survivors, only to find those I met killed before my eyes by Xenos. General Korolev then told me that N.O.V.A.
had seen enough and ordered me to go to the Computer Security Deck to set the ship to self-destruct. I travelled to a computer room,
where I recieved the self destruct codes for the ship. Then, during my mission, I was contacted by Prometheus, a computer AI that
gained sentience through a quantum viral accident. He revealed that all is not what it seemed, and not to trust Colonial Administration.


I had to go into the vaccum of space to go from place to place on the damaged ship, using storage compartments along the way to restore the
oxygen supply in my suit. Yelena discovered that the thrusters were on turbocharge mode, and they would reach New Ceres before I got to
the Self Destruct Computer, so I went to the room with the auxiliary drives.

Yelena uploaded a virus that shut the engines down, but I had to stay and guard them to make sure no one else turned them back on. I then
resumed my journey to the Computer Security Deck, where the self-destruct sequence could be activated. Yelena told me to evacuate after
I turned on the self destruct sequence, but Prometheus told me to go to the Navigation Room and fix the evacuation system so the other
marines still on the ship could escape. After I did this I escaped in a N.O.V.A. Shuttle.



New Ceres Outer Jungle

I then landed the escape shuttle on New Ceres, in the outer jungle, and fought my way through attacking Xenos to nearby N.O.V.A. forces
who'd set up a temporary area to hold out against the Xenos, with a satellite and a few crates of supplies, including ammunition. The leader
of the group directed me to the Epsilon Bunker, which hadn't been used since "Rebellion Times". Soon after departing, I came across a
large Xeno Portal on a helipad outside an old base, through which Xeno troops arrived. A N.O.V.A. shuttle pilot then contacted me, asking me to destroy the Power Crystals that powered the portal so that I could land the shuttle on the helipad it occupied.


In exchange, the pilot offered to clear debris that was preventing me from advancing further. I did as asked, and the pilot made good his word,
destroying the debris.I then fought through countless more Xenos, and soon recieved a distress call from Bravo Lead requesting assistance
for Bravo squad to hold the perimeter outside Bunker One (the Epsilon Bunker). Yelena asked me to proceed to the bunker immediately, and
I did so. Shortly before reaching the bunker, Prometheus contacted me, informing me that, after accessing the military database, I found out
that records indicated a second cargo ship flew with the Colonial Pride before I was sent there, yet there was no information on what happened
to it.



Epsilon Bunker

By the time I arrived, the area outside the bunker was overrun by Xeno hostiles. I neutralized those attacking me, then made for the bunker
entrance, where a Bravo Squad member informed me that they had been pushed back to "square one", and asked me to grab the nearby
machine gun turret to defend the position while they hacked the door circuits to retreat inside. Presumably the base was in lockdown,
either due to N.O.V.A. procedure or Xeno sabotage. The position was successfuly held regardless, and they retreated inside.


Despite this, Fiends breached the entrance and were shot down by an anonymous marine who told me he was expected and sent me on his way.
A second marine in the following room directed me to Command HQ in the lower levels of the base, and within seconds the main power generator
was shut down by Xeno saboteurs. The marine then directed me to "grab Bonesy and Clydesdale for backup" in the lower level and restart the generator.
Once down there, I was given a less than friendly welcome by the two, with Bonesy mocking his return to N.O.V.A. and Clydesdale expressing mistrust.
The two were shortly killed by Fiends in a Xeno ambush.

I then made my way to the generator. In the main generator room, I deactivated a large portal through which Xeno troops came, like the one on the helipad
in the outer jungle, by destroying its Power Crystals. The main generator couldn't be restarted, so I continued through the bunker until I met two other marines
who directed me to an elevator to the backup generator. While on the elevator, a handful of Fiends came through a portal in a failed ambush attempt. After
navigating my way through the debris from a crashed N.O.V.A. shuttle in the next room, I then proceeded through a corridor to a large Xeno-controlled room.
There, I eliminated the Xenos I could, took a machine gun turret from a drone I killed and defended the area, then neutralized remaining hostiles on foot.
I soon reached the backup generator, killed drones defending the room and activated it. I was then congratulated by a N.O.V.A. marine via helmet
communications and ordered to take the elevator to Command HQ to recieve a mission from General Korolev.

I reported to Korolev as ordered, however not face-to-face. They spoke with two N.O.V.A. guards preventing me from getting near and Korolev sat in a
chair facing a computer screen away from me. Korolev ordered me to prevent the Xenos from launching the bunker's nuclear warhead they'd secured by
hacking its systems. I then entered the rocket chamber, neutralized attacking Xenos and took an elevator up to rocket control, where Yelena informed me
the missile was armed and ready to launch. I then hacked the control computer and averted the launch. Behind me, theAlien Abomination rose on an
elevator to the control level. It then attacked me, but thanks to Yelena's advice I killed it by disabling its legs and shooting its brain.

I then took the elevator back to the surface, where Yelena had arranged for a 4x4 to meet me and drive me from the bunker and out of the forest to a
helipad where a N.O.V.A. shuttle was waiting for him.

New Ceres Mining Facility

I crash landed the N.O.V.A. shuttle in a New Ceres N.O.V.A. mining facility in order to investigate it. Almost immediately after exiting the landing site,
I was attacked by a sniper, who I in return shot with a sniper rifle of my own, killing the hostile. I then proceeded, but the door I wished to go through was
locked, so Yelena directed me to a nearby control console. Yelena commented that "security got tight since she last checked the place".

Upon closer inspection , Yelena commented that while the occupants of the facility were human marines, they were controlled by
Xenobiotic Parasites on their chests. The Xenos needed reinforcements for their invasion, so used human host bodies. Yelena
ordered me to consider them hostiles and kill on sight. I complied, and then activated the control console, unlocking the door.
Yelena was curious to find out why the Xenos took the mining facility, and the pair soon found out - a portal was placed at the
end of the ore cart transport system, sending minerals to where the Xenos came from to support their invasion.
Yelena claimed that if the facility was lost to the Xenos, humanity wouldn't survive further Xeno attacks, and ordered me to get to the central mining station
to stop production by any means necessary.

I left the area and neutralized any Xenos on the way. I then navigated a snowstorm using red beacons along the path, as the storm scrambled Yelena's GPS system.
When the storm ended, I founded another locked door, so again used a nearby control console and killed any Xenos in my way, soon ending up in the area where the
main computer console that controlled all mining operations was located. Yelena commented that it was strangely quiet, then I accessed the console, uploading
a virus to it. As soon as I stepped outside of the building, Yelena detected multiple hostiles closing in - it was an ambush!!! I used his Sniper Rifle to repel
hostiles, but by the time the area was clear, the Xenos had already restarted production from the backup computer.

On the way to the backup computer, Prometheus warned me that I was only chosen to destroy the Colonial Pride because of my reputation as a "loose cannon".
There was no official record of me being re-instated as a marine, either - I was a perfect scapegoat if things went wrong. Prometheus then warned me that maybe
I will in danger when the Ceresian Government no longer needed me. Straight after this, I recieved a plea for help from 3 surviving N.O.V.A. marines that hadn't
been infected by the Xeno parasite but were trapped in a building. General Korolev ordered me to ignore them and proceed with my mission, but I freed them
anyway, and they expressed their thanks. I then went on my way, killed all Xenos in my path and found the building housing the backup computer. I then uploaded
a virus to it, disabling the computer system and the entire facility, stopping the transport of minerals to support the Xenos.

I then proceeded outside, and Yelena told him Prometheus contacted her, and that I had to listen to him - it was humanity's only chance for survival. She then said
she would be deleted for saying what she did, and I was in great danger - Prometheus would explain. Indeed, very soon afterwards she was deleted.
Governor Lysenko then contacted me, congratulating me and saying he sent troops to rendezvous with me and requesting me to stay put. In reality, these troops
would either kill or capture and convict me for crimes I didn't commit. As it so happened, a portal appeared by me, which Prometheus told me to go through,
and I did - as a means to escape Lysenko's men and stop the Xenos for good.

Final Judgement

When I appeared on the other side of the portal, I was in the outskirts of a monolithic city on the aliens travelling asteroid. Prometheus explained that he
needed information on why the Xenos attacked to stop them, so I began searching the city for answers. Very soon, I found a Plasma Gun, making killing powerful
Xenos much easier.I also later came across Xeno Sentinels for the first time. Eventually, I began to find terminals, which I hacked and Prometheus deciphered.
The first terminal had repeated references to a "central monolithic tower". I then left the terminal in search of others, and came across purple crystals,
which absorbed power from wepons and emitted it in the form of a shockwave - useful for crowd control, but dangerous.

A while into my's time on the alien citadel, just before I entered the central monolothic tower, Prometheus finally told a suspicious me how he
became self-conscious in 2129 and used to be a normal N.O.V.A. SPECIAL OPS AI.


Once I was inside the central tower,I fought through many Xenos, including infected marines and many Heavy Demons, which are quite hard to kill
compared to other Xenos. I also uncovered more terminals. The first of these referred to other terminals and spoke of not one, but two alien races.
One race, literally "They Who Judge", an ancient race living mostly in a hibernation state - this was the uncovering of the Judger race, and that the
Xenos were mere Caretakers.The second of these revealed that the second vessel that travelled with the Colonial Pride crashed on the asteroid,
and the automated protection system regarded it as a threat, ordering the Caretakers to eradicate the race responsible - humanity. The final terminal
located the automated protection system - inside the tower.

I made my way towards the archway leading to where the automated protection system was located and hacked the terminal meant to activate it, but the circuit was almost devoid of energy, so I had to hold his position until it opened. When I did, I found a large shaft leading deep within the tower - where I wanted to go. As such, Prometheus told me to jump and I did. Finally at the heart of the invasion force, I entered the chamber of the Automated Command Core (automated protection system), and was attacked by it. I retaliated by destroying the Power Crystals it used to replenish itself, then the crystals embedded in it. Heavily damaged, it retreated, and finally, the Judgers woke from their hibernation.


The Judgers referred to me as "advocate", and deemed Prometheus worthy of being the eternal guardian of humanity. They also restored
Yelena to life and vowed that humanity's corrupt leaders shall be "rendered unto oblivion" for their selfish pursuit of power. They also commended
me for saving my peers on the Colonial Pride and New Ceres.

N.O.V.A. 2

I lived happily on Scorpius after the Judger incident, away from N.O.V.A., but still with the resurrected Yelena. Life remained peaceful
until the truth became apparent - once again humanity faced an alien threat, and there was one man best suited to stop it - me.

Second Retirement

After the Judger incident, I went into hiding, spending my second retirement in a farming colony on Scorpius. For six years I lived peacefully
and the villagers helped to hide me, until my village was attacked by government troops and the survivors hid in Judger ruins there. The village leader
contacted me for help while I was elsewhere shooting bottles, and I rushed to the scene, saving the leader from a pair of Controlled Marines. I then sent the
leader into the ruins with the others, and held off attacks with a Turret Placer and the other weapons I could find.

When the attack was over, Prometheus contacted me, who was a "crucial part" of his plan. I then claimed the Son of Victory and Prometheus set me
on course for Aquarius to sabotage a war factory there. He also told me of a newly discovered alien group known as the Volterites that recently formed
an alliance with a few colonist politicians, the leaders being The Overseer and President Gerard Folsom. A hologram of them was shown by Prometheus,
revealing their goal... to eliminate Earth's forces, N.O.V.A. troops included, and be the dominant force in known space.

Arrival On Aquarius

The ship was then attacked by Volterites above Aquarius, which I dealt with. In the process an explosion was caused, ripping a hole in the ship.
I jumped through this hole, firing my assault rifle to propel myself into the planet's gravity well. Succeeding, I fell through the atmosphere unscathed
thanks to the protection of my Mobile Armord Suit, and when I was just about to land attempted to activate the in-built rocket boosters in my boots
to break my fall, but failed, when I landed, I was directly in front of a Human-Volterite Alliance Base, more specifically, the Sigma Outpost.

The Son of Victory, no longer under attack and now driven by Yelena, followed me, hovering by the beach until it was needed. Meanwhile, I killed the hostiles outside and hijacked a speeder bike to breach the base, driving through a window. I then cleared the inside of the base, and was making my way to the Transport Highway outside when a Mech broke in and attacked me, driving me outside. Luckily a rocket launcher was at hand on the back of a nearby 4x4, which I used to destroy the Mech. Unfortunately, just after I did so, two more appeared. I managed to destroy one, then the Son Of Victory appeared overhead and destroyed the other. Further loaded Rocket Launchers scattered around allowed me to retrieve replacement ammunition.

I then left the Sigma Outpost for the entrance to the Transport Highway leading to the Human-Volterite Alliance War Factory which I needed to
sabotage in order to prevent the Alliance amassing an army large enough to crush those of Earth. Yelena proceeded to pilot the Son of Victory
away from the area, having been engaged by two battle cruisers. With no air support, I had to destroy incoming hostiles, 4x4s included, using my
remaining Rocket Launcher ammunition and other weapons.

Each time I approached a security gateway, Alliance troops activated the forcefield in it, preventing me from proceeding further until I dealt with his
attackers, at which point the forcefield deactivated. This happened a few times, until I reached a second outpost. I passed through the exterior without
entering and was fairly quickly on my way after dealing with hostiles. The pattern continued and I soon reached a gateway with an entry slope either side
and a machine gun on top, which I used to disperse further hostiles quickly.

After this, an Archangel Dropship dropped of a Mech, which I dealt with, then continued to the other side of the gateway. Here, I navigated through various
crates and dealt with further troops until I reached a clear bridge up ahead. However, Yelena hit one of the enemy cruisers with the Son Of Victory and warned
it was coming down, as it did, destroying the bridge.

Because of the destruction of the bridge, I had to take a detour through the jungle below, filled with aggressive flora. I navigated through it and shortly reached
a waterfall. Yelena explained she made a slight elevation error while mapping the route, and suggested me jump - which I did.

Human-Volterite Allince War Factory

I landed in a pool of water by the War Factory entrance. I killed the guards, then hacked the door terminal, allowing Yelena to open the entrance, extract floor
plans and upload a virus intended to disable the Factory AI. Yelena directed me to the Command Centre to shut down the production core, but as I entered the
facility the Factory AI, driven crazy by the virus, initiated the Shut-Down sequence, locking down the building and attempting to kill anyone inside.

Once inside, I went to a 4x4 production area, where I used a turret on an unfinished 4x4 to fend off hostiles. I then went through a corridor of dangerous
energy beams caused by the insane factory AI, disabling them with my EMP blast to pass. In this corridor was the corpse of a Skull Crusher, presumably
killed by the energy beams. I picked up its Melee Disc after Yelena mentioned it could be useful.

I continued through the facility, coming across an unfinished Mech and restarting its assembly line so I could use the finished vehicle, as I did. I blasted my
way to the courtyard, which was filled with Controlled Marines carrying Rocket Launchers, Mechs and explosive barrels. I made my way through it and
left it in another part of the facility near to the Command Centre, which I made my way to. I found some mines and picked them up on the way, saving them
to blow up the Production Core. When at the Command Center, the Core AI said "I am afraid" before being severely damaged by me, initiating the factory
self-destruct and unlocking emergency exit routes. I took the nearest elevator to the surface, and on my way Yelena reported that the Son of Victory was disabled
and taken by a tractor beam along with her to a scientific base nearby. I made my way there, holding a surviving Controlled Marine outside hostage and making
him drive me to the Niumbus Scientific Base on a 4x4.

On the way to the base, I was attacked by many hostile 4x4s, which I destroyed. Eventually I reached a bridge, which was up so I couldn't go any further. As such,
I placed a mine on the hostage driver's chest as incentive to stay where I was, then left the 4x4 and fighting my way to the control console on the top floor, where
I used it to lower the bridge. By the console, a set of jetpack armor was on display, causing me to comment on my hate of flying.

When I got back, the driver was still waiting for me and helped with a quick escape from the area. They proceeded to the gateway area to the main base, where
I again wiped out hostiles and used a nearby console, this time to open the gate. When this was done, I freed the driver of the mine placed on him, telling him to
start running, to which he gave a salute and ran from me. The mine was then used to blow up the no longer needed 4x4.
